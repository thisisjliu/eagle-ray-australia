Eagle Ray Australia is an international timber trading company specialising in the procurement and export of pine and plantation softwood. We source industrial-grade timber directly from certified sawmills and move it to manufacturers across Asia Pacific and beyond.

Our clients are primarily pallet, skid and crate manufacturers who depend on consistent volume, on-spec material, and reliable delivery. We manage the full trade cycle — sourcing, logistics, documentation — so our clients don't have to.

Our Approach

We are brokers. Here's why that matters.

Eagle Ray Australia is a professional timber broker. We connect industrial buyers — primarily pallet, skid and crate manufacturers — with certified sawmill suppliers across Asia, Australasia and beyond. Our role is to find the right supply source, at the right specification and price, and coordinate everything that follows.

Buyers choose a broker over going direct for the same reason companies hire specialists: the supply chain is our core competency, not a distraction from theirs. We carry the supplier relationships, market intelligence, compliance handling and logistics coordination — so our clients can focus on manufacturing.

What We Stand For

The principles we trade by.

01

Specification as standard.

Every shipment meets the agreed specification before we confirm loading. If it doesn't, we don't ship it. That's the minimum we hold ourselves to.

02

Relationships over volume.

We don't chase tonnage. We build partnerships. Our buyers know our team by name and deal with people who understand their supply chain.

03

Direct access to our team.

When you enquire, you're talking to the people who sourced the timber and arranged the freight. No handoffs, no scripts.

04

One standard. Every market.

We apply the same quality threshold regardless of destination — no exceptions based on geography or order size.
