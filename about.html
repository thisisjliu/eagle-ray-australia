Our Story

A Family Business Built on Australian Timber

Eagle Ray Australia was founded in 2008 by a family with deep roots in the timber industry and a strong connection to Taiwan's manufacturing sector. What began as a modest export operation has grown into a trusted supply channel for pallet, skid and wooden crate producers across Taiwan.

The name Eagle Ray reflects the company's character — the eagle ray moves with purpose and precision through open water, much like our business navigates the complex international timber trade. Grounded in Australian values of fairness and reliability, we have spent nearly two decades forging relationships that endure.

Today we remain proudly family-owned, with the same founding principles guiding every shipment — quality timber, honest dealings and on-time delivery.
