About Eagle Ray

We connect the full timber trade chain.

Eagle Ray Australia operates as a professional timber broker — sitting between sawmill producers and industrial manufacturers across global markets. We buy from mills that need consistent, long-term volume off-take and sell to manufacturers who need reliable specification and supply.

For sawmill producers, we provide a dependable off-take partner with direct access to overseas buyers they can't reach independently. For manufacturers — primarily pallet, skid and crate producers — we handle procurement, specification, and the full export chain from mill gate to receiving dock.

Beyond timber, Eagle Ray holds strategic property investments — a deliberate extension of the same approach to capital: asset-backed, long-term, with clear return criteria.

We work with both sides of the timber trade — sawmill producers and industrial buyers — managing procurement, logistics, and export so each party can focus on their core business.

Timber Procurement

We source pine and plantation softwood on behalf of industrial manufacturers — primarily pallet, skid and crate producers. Consistent specification, reliable volume, competitive pricing. We negotiate directly with certified sawmills so you don't have to manage the supply chain.

Export & Logistics
