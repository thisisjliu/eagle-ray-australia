About Eagle Ray

We are timber brokers.

Eagle Ray Australia operates as a professional timber broker — connecting industrial buyers with sawmill suppliers across global markets. We don't own mills or forests. We own deep supply chain knowledge and the relationships that make it work.

Our clients are pallet, skid and crate manufacturers who need consistent volume and reliable specification. We identify the right supply source, negotiate the terms, and coordinate the full logistics chain — from the mill gate to your receiving dock.
