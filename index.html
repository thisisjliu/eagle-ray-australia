What We Do

Connecting Australian Hardwood to Taiwan's Industrial Supply Chain

We source and export industrial-grade timber from across Australia, delivering consistent quality to manufacturers who build pallets, skids and wooden crates for Taiwan's logistics and manufacturing sectors.

As a family business, we maintain direct relationships with both our timber suppliers and our buyers — ensuring traceability, reliability and competitive pricing at every shipment.
