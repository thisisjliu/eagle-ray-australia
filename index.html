About Eagle Ray

We connect the full timber trade chain.

Eagle Ray Australia operates as a professional timber broker — sitting between sawmill producers and industrial manufacturers across global markets. We buy from mills that need consistent, long-term volume off-take and sell to manufacturers who need reliable specification and supply.

For sawmill producers, we provide a dependable off-take partner with direct access to overseas buyers they can't reach independently. For manufacturers, we handle procurement, specification, and the full export chain from mill gate to receiving dock.

Beyond timber, Eagle Ray holds strategic property investments — a deliberate extension of the same approach to capital: asset-backed, long-term, with clear return criteria.
